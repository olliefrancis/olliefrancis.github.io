Hi, I'm Ollie Francis. I'm a UX consultant, product designer and mentor based in Bristol, UK

I remove fear and help change behaviour around technology so that the people I work with can be more pragmatic and embrace change. I enable leaders and their teams to build more user-centred digital products that benefit society and human culture.

I founded UX design consultancy Deckchair with Becky Taylor. We work with teams to discover user needs and define business problems. I rapidly build prototypes to test with real users in days rather than weeks. My aim is to create a safe space for teams to thrive.

I have worked with teams on digital products and services for the NHS, Department of Health, University of Reading, H&M and the UK Space Agency.

I mentor individuals and teams looking to improve their UX process and practice. I've worked with leaders to change organisational culture and helped lots of people find a new path in their careers.

Like many designers, I'm a frustrated artist at heart, exploring the world through talks, writing and making, to express myself. I am currently very interested in using game dynamics and the blockchain to save the planet.

Products I've designed

  • Presto Music - A specialist music streaming service built for classical and jazz lovers, made by classical and jazz lovers.

  • Untagged Fashion - An app that uses gaming dynamics to swap old clothes for tokens, so you can get something new to you and help slow down fast fashion.

  • Sparkyard - Music streaming service and educational resource for schools, to get kids learning and thriving by singing.

  • EnergyMetric - SaaS-based modelling platform that helps governments and energy companies to better predict the yields of renewables.
